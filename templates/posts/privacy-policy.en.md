# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: November 2025*

Welcome to **TheChatbot**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services via WhatsApp.

## 1. Information We Collect

When interacting with our service, we collect the following types of information:

- **Identity Data**: Your name and phone number, as provided by WhatsApp.
- **Authentication Data**: Google access tokens (Access and Refresh Tokens) to authenticate you and access Google services on your behalf.
- **Communication Data**: The history of your messages sent to TheChatbot.
- **Usage Data**: IDs of Google Sheets spreadsheets you configure for expense tracking.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide the conversational service via WhatsApp.
- Authenticate your identity using Google Accounts.
- Manage your financial data in your own Google Sheets upon your request.
- Process your messages using Artificial Intelligence to provide relevant responses.

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with specific providers to facilitate our service:

- **Meta (WhatsApp)**: For sending and receiving messages.
- **Google**: For authentication and access to your spreadsheets. We securely store tokens to maintain this connection.
- **OpenAI**: We send message content to OpenAI to generate intelligent responses. Please refer to OpenAI's data policy for more details.

## 4. Data Retention and Security

Your data, including chat logs and authentication tokens, is securely stored in our database. We retain this information to preserve the context of your conversations and ensure the continued operation of services such as expense tracking.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data directly through the chatbot commands or by contacting us.

## 6. Google User Data

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.
